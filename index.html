Bio

I am an Associate Professor of Civil Engineering at Suleyman Demirel University, specializing in the innovative integration of Transportation Infrastructure and Artificial Intelligence.

My research is dedicated to transforming traditional engineering with AI-driven precision. I focus on developing advanced predictive models using Physics-Informed Machine Learning, optimizing urban traffic flow through Monte Carlo simulations, and enhancing road safety with high-accuracy crash severity forecasting.

Driven by a vision of sustainable and smart mobility, my work aligns with UN Sustainable Development Goals (Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure & Sustainable Cities). I strive to build the bridge between data science and physical infrastructure to create safer, more efficient, and resilient transportation networks for the future.
