Bio

I am an Associate Professor in Civil Engineering, deeply passionate about the intersection of transportation infrastructure and artificial intelligence. My work focuses on developing predictive models and traffic management systems using Python, Colab, and advanced machine learning algorithms.

I strive to bridge the gap between traditional civil engineering practices and modern AI-driven solutions, optimizing urban mobility, safety, and sustainability.

Research & AI Modeling

Here are some of my active research areas and computational models:

  • Traffic Flow Optimization: SUMO-based simulations combined with AI methodologies.
  • Pavement Condition & AI: Predictive maintenance models analyzing roughness and driving quality using advanced algorithms.
  • Crash Severity Prediction: Utilizing data science and deep learning to achieve Vision Zero goals in urban networks.
