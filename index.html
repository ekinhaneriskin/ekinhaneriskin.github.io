Bio

I am an Associate Professor in Civil Engineering at Suleyman Demirel University, deeply passionate about the intersection of transportation infrastructure and artificial intelligence.

My work focuses on developing predictive models and traffic management systems using advanced machine learning algorithms. I strive to bridge the gap between traditional civil engineering practices and modern AI-driven solutions, optimizing urban mobility, safety, and sustainability.

Research & Citations

Active research areas include Traffic Flow Optimization, Pavement Condition AI Modeling, and Crash Severity Prediction.
